# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

B2B procurement managers, engineers, and manufacturing directors at European and Asian electronics companies. Evaluating the site from a desktop browser to assess supplier credibility before making contact. No leisure — they are deciding whether to trust.

## Product Purpose

Generate qualified inquiries for Zbroya, a Czech-registered trading company supplying electronic components. The site's job is to communicate reliability, precision, and European-market legitimacy before the user sends an email. Success is a contact request from a qualified manufacturer.

## Brand Personality

Precise, direct, serious. Professional without stiffness. European industrial confidence — a company that knows exactly what it does and does not oversell.

## Anti-references

HubSpot-style SaaS: rounded friendliness, testimonial carousels, gradient CTAs, "scale your business" copy.
Mass e-commerce: urgency patterns, discount badges, product-grid layouts.
Startup landing pages: illustrated mascots, pastel palettes, "we're disrupting X" tone.

## Design Principles

Weight earns trust — heaviness in motion signals substance, not speed.
Precision over decoration — every element has a structural reason to exist.
Earned confidence — state facts, let the design carry the credibility, never assert it.
European industrial register — restrained, structured, no flourish for flourish's sake.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. prefers-reduced-motion respected: all animations disabled or instant when set.
